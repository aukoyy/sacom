# Hverdagshelten app

**Role:** UX Designer  
**Type:** Course exam at Noroff autumn/winter 2025
**Tools:** Figma, FigJam, Canva

---

A mobile app that makes it easier for people in financially vulnerable situations to find free activities, food distribution, and local support.

# Background

**The challenge**

Information about free support in Norway is scattered across websites, Facebook groups, and posters. This makes it stressful and time-consuming for people to find help when they need it.
